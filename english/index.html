This picture gives information about a young woman and shopping.

According to the picture, the young woman is wearing a blue dress and carrying a pink bag.

The picture also shows that the young woman is looking at the pineapple.

I think she must feel engrossed.

This reminds me of a documentary about people and shopping one year ago when i was in school because it is such a memorable experience.

Before I wached that documentary, I used to think that it was humdrum. Although there were quite lots of documentary genres in china, it had never attracted my attention before.

I had never watched this kind of documentries before, and this one caught my attention immediately.It gave me a chance to expose myself to people and shopping and changed my point of view on certain things, so i can expand my horizon.It was this documentary that completely changed my perspective about people and shopping.

In conclusion, this picture is very informative and helps me to get a better understanding of people and shopping.
